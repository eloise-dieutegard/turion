Turion Radio is a small radiophonic laboratory, sound experimentation, nomadic station and archive platform.
The ephemeral radio takes shapes around projects/contexts and transmits live (and on 108Mhz if you are closeby) during events that we attend from time to time.
If you have any ideas, content to you want to share, collaborate... contact us at radioturion@proton.me and we'll try respond more or less quickly !
The audio flux is provided by the best team P-node <3
