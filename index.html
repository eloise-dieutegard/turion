Turion Radio is a small and nomadic radiophonic experimentation sort of lab and archive platform.
The ephemeral radio takes shapes around projects and spaces it landed on and transmits live (and on 108Mhz if you are closeby) from time to time.
If you have any ideas, content to you want to share, collaborate... contact us at radioturion(at)proton.me and we'll try respond more or less quickly !
The audio flux is provided by the best team P-node <3
