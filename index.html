Turion Radio is a small radiophonic laboratory, sound experimentation, nomadic station and archive platform.
The ephemeral radio takes shapes around projects/contexts and transmits live during events that we attend from time to time.
If you have ideas of content to you want to share, collaborate... contact us at radioturion@proton.me and we'll try respond more or less quickly !
The audio flux is provided by the best team P-node <3
